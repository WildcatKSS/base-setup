## Strategic Principles

- Paying customers over features
- Small paid MVP over broad free product
- Maintainability over cleverness
- Security by default
- Privacy by design
- Measurable progress over vague progress

## Non-Negotiables

- Must be B2B SaaS unless explicitly changed by ADR
- Must be buildable by one developer
- Must be maintainable without a large team
- Must have realistic path to €1.000 MRR within 12 months
- Must avoid ad-dependent business models
- Must avoid marketplace/network-effect dependency for MVP
