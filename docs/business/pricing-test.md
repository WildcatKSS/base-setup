# Pricing Test

## Hypothesis

Customers will pay €TBD/month for solving [problem].

## Price Points

| Price | Target Segment | Expected Objection |
|---:|---|---|
| €9 | Very small users | Too limited |
| €29 | Small businesses | Need proof |
| €79 | Professional users | ROI required |
| €199 | Teams | Procurement |

## Test Questions

- Would this be a personal/team/company expense?
- What would make this obviously worth paying for?
- At what price would this feel expensive but acceptable?
- At what price would you not take it seriously?
- Would you pre-order or join a paid pilot?
