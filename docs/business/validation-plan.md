# Validation Plan

## Objective

Validate willingness to pay before building.

## Minimum Evidence Required

- 10 customer interviews
- 5 confirmed painful-problem responses
- 2 strong payment-intent signals
- 1 pilot/pre-order/paid-beta signal or clear buying process

## Methods

- Customer interviews
- Cold outreach
- Landing page
- Pricing test
- Competitor pricing research

## Pass/Fail Criteria

Pass only if evidence suggests a realistic path to first paying customers.
