# Customer Interview Script

## Opening

I'm researching how [target customer] handles [problem]. I'm not selling anything today; I want to understand the workflow.

## Questions

1. How do you currently handle [problem]?
2. How often does this happen?
3. What happens if it is not solved?
4. What does it cost in time, money, or risk?
5. What tools or services do you use today?
6. What do you dislike about them?
7. Who decides whether to buy a solution?
8. What budget would this come from?
9. If this problem disappeared, what would that be worth?
10. Can I follow up with a prototype?

## Strong Signal

- They ask when it is available
- They mention budget
- They offer introduction to decision-maker
- They agree to pilot
- They describe current workaround as painful
